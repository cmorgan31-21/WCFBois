Tiebreaker: players tied on points are ranked by whoever had a team run deepest into the tournament (Champion > Final > Semis > Quarters > R16 > R32). Tied rows show a ⚖ badge. And note: Runner-Up earns 0 — champion or bust.
The tier multiplier applies to each game and is rounded per game, then summed. So a Tier 3 win is always 5 pts, a Tier 3 draw always 2 pts — the parts always add up to the team total.
▼ How is 460 the max?
Each team earns at most 9 from groups (3 wins × 3 pts) + 5 (R32) + 8 (R16) + 12 (QF) + 16 (SF) + 30 (Champion) = 80 raw. Runner-up (20) and Champion (30) are mutually exclusive. By tier: T1×1.0=80, T2×1.25=100, T3×1.5=120, T4×2.0=160. 80+100+120+160 = 460.
